The Beatles were an English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960, comprising John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr. They are regarded as the most influential band of all time and were integral to the development of 1960s counterculture and the recognition of popular music as an art form.   • John Lennon

    was born in Liverpool, Merseyside, England on October 9, 1940. Lennon's parents separated when he was four. Lennon was reared by his aunt, but his mother visited often and encouraged his interest in music. Lennon's mother Julia was killed in a car accident in 1958. In 1962, Lennon married Cynthia Powell. Their son Julian was named after Lennon's mother. The couple divorced in 1968, and in 1969 Lennon married Yoko Ono. On December 8, 1980, John Lennon was shot and killed by a deranged fan outside his apartment in New York City.

  • Paul McCartney

    Paul McCartney was born in Liverpool. McCartney was born on June 18, 1942, and he credits his pianist father as an early musical influence. McCartney's mother died when he was fourteen. He wrote "When I'm Sixty-Four" when he was sixteen with an eye toward selling the piece to Frank Sinatra. McCartney married Linda Eastman in 1969, and the couple was still married when she died in 1998. He married Heather Mills briefly and is now married to Nancy Shevell.

  • George Harrison

    George Harrison was born in Liverpool on February 25, 1943. Elvis Presley, Carl Perkins, Little Richard, and Buddy Holly were early musical influences. In 1966, he married Pattie Boyd, who later left him for his friend Eric Clapton. Although Lennon and McCartney wrote most of the songs, George Harrison wrote "Here Comes the Sun". In 1978, Harrison married Olivia Arias. George Harrison died of cancer on November 29, 2001.

  • Ringo Starr

    Ringo Starr was born in Liverpool on July 7, 1940. Originally named Richard Starkey, he spent a year in the hospital when he was six as a result of childhood illnesses. He first played percussion in a patient band formed by hospital staff. Starr's parents divorced when he was four, and his mother remarried when he was thirteen. Starr's stepfather was an early supporter of his musical interests. In 1965, Starr married Maureen Cox; the couple divorced in 1975. In 1980, Starr married Barbara Bach.
